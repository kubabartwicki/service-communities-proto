Create a step by step: step by step

The steps you need to go through to make a step-by-step guide on GOV.UK.

  1. Step 1: Identify a candidate service for a step-by-step

    1. Choose a service you think will benefit from a step-by-step

  2. Step 2: Scope the end-to-end service or services

    You need to make sure the service has been scoped right. You'll need to look at the whole landscape and check that it's not too big or too small.

    See the 'Start a business' service landscape


  3. Step 3: Map the user journey

    Get the right people in the room and map the user journey

    See the agenda for the 'Start a business' step-by-step user journey mapping workshop


  4. Step 4: Create a draft step-by-step

  5. Step 5: Get a GOV.UK content designer to review it

  6. Step 5: Get all the stakeholders involved in delivering the service to review it

  7. Step 5: Publish and monitor the live content
